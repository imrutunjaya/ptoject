Government Exam Prep

Study Notes for Government Exams

1. General Awareness

  • Important current affairs: National & International news of past 6 months.
  • Indian Constitution - fundamental rights, duties, articles.
  • History - freedom movement leaders, major events.
  • Geography - important rivers, mountains, states & capitals.

2. Quantitative Aptitude

  • Number systems and simplification techniques.
  • Ratio, proportion, percentage, averages.
  • Time, speed and distance, work and time problems.
  • Algebra basics and important formulas.

3. Reasoning Ability

  • Seating arrangement and puzzles practice.
  • Blood relations, directions, coding-decoding.
  • Analogy and series problems.
  • Syllogism and logical deduction.

4. English Language

  • Reading comprehension practice regularly.
  • Vocabulary building: Synonyms, antonyms, idioms.
  • Grammar basics: Tenses, parts of speech, error spotting.
  • Sentence improvement and para jumbles.

5. Exam Tips

  • Practice time management during mock tests.
  • Focus on accuracy to avoid negative marking.
  • Revise frequently and use flashcards for memory.
  • Stay calm and maintain a healthy study routine.
